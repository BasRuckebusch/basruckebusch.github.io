About

Hello! I'm Bas Ruckebusch.

I’m a game developer with a passion for exploring the nuts and bolts of how things work. My main strengths lie in tackling complex algorithms, whether it’s AI pathfinding, terrain generation, gesture recognition, or any other system that make games tick. I thrive on experimentation, always eager to dive deep into new challenges or unfamiliar territory.

My work spans a wide range of projects from full games to custom tools and even my own engine. I work best when I have constraints to creatively bend. Working within limitations (like developing for the Nintendo DS) pushes me to think creatively and find clever solutions. I love rapid iteration, refining ideas until they’re the best they can be.

Collaboration is a big part of how I work. I enjoy being part of a team, lifting others up, and feeding off constant feedback to create something great together.

In my free time I enjoy TTRPGS, writing and making pictures out of tiny squares.
